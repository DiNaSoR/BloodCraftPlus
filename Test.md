Imperivm Draconis

 ‚Äî 2025-12-27 09:33 PM
Outside of the readme and your config, there is nothing to read, some servers make their own guides but there is no universal guide - you hit max level you prestige by running the respective prestige command for [experience] which is pre-requesite for [exo] (prestiging for exo form) and you prestige for [blood legacy] for each blood-type same rather than conventional leveling & killing like 'experience' you need "to bite to kill" or kill and feed on V Bloods to grind up "essence" which works like xp for said "blood legacy" and run the respective command for blood each time you hit lvl 100 in blood, stats with a synergy for your class will acquire a 20% bonus at max lvl (can be higher depending upon your settings). Then you have [weapon expertise] same principle as blood expect you can just freely use said weapons on whatever targets with your weapon just level your weapons to max level and run the respective prestige command for weapons stats are respective for "weapon expertise" and will also acquire a bonus at maximum leveled prestige for weaponry. Lastly are Familiars, the bonus of which is very small as of v1.1 due to internal changes to the game so unless you gear them up and max out their respective prestige you won't see much of a change.

The bonus stats from synergies for a class are for all spells of your class's spell school, or just for the ones that you can equip as a "shift spell" ?

Imperivm Draconis

 ‚Äî 2026-01-15 09:48 PM
Shift spells sourced from AB_groups (NPCs) do not scale from stats such as SP they have fixed values, the only thing they're influenced by are spell gems if using standard spells. The stats for for class synergies are only applied via specific stats for specific classes as 20% bonus to effects for example: MaxHP on the BK & VL, SP and SCR on the ArcaneSorc, etc. They apply to all equipped weapons and standard spells if related to Primary/Physical/Spell/Leech where applicable for each one.
